Adjacency

Implement the ability to detect whether two rectangles are adjacent. Adjacency is defined as the sharing of a side. Side sharing may be proper or sub-line, where a sub-line share is one where one side of rectangle A is a line that exists as a set of points wholly contained on some other side of rectangle B.(Click and drag small box, drop close to edge of big box to snap.)
